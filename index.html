Meet the Candidate

A Lifelong Public Servant & Cultural Advocate

Scott N. Bighorse is a proud member of the Osage Nation with decades of experience in tribal and state government. Born and raised in Pawhuska, Oklahoma, he is a member of the BigHorse and Pearson families and belongs to the Wa-Xa-Ko-Li'n District.

His leadership is grounded in Osage Values — respect, accountability, and service — and shaped by a deep commitment to preserving the Nation's sovereignty and building a future where all Osages can thrive.

Collaborative Leadership

Scott leads with an open door and an open mind. He believes leadership is not about power — it's about service. His approach is collaborative, respectful, and rooted in the belief that every Osage citizen and employee deserves to be heard, valued, and empowered.

Osage Heritage

As a member of the BigHorse and Pearson families, Scott carries forward generations of Osage tradition. His leadership honors the past while preparing for the future, ensuring cultural preservation remains at the heart of governance.
